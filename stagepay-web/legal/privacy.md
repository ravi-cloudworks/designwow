# StagePay — Privacy Policy

**Beta Version — Last updated: [DATE]**

This Privacy Policy explains what information StagePay ("we", "us", "our") collects from you when you use StagePay (the "Service"), how we use it, and your rights regarding it. This Policy applies to users in India and is intended to be read together with our [Terms & Conditions](./terms.md).

---

## 1. Information We Collect

**From Google Sign-In:** When you sign in with Google, we receive your name, email address, and profile photo. We never receive or store your Google password.

**From your waitlist application:** the role you select, your Instagram profile URL, your YouTube channel URL (if provided), and a short description of the work you do.

**Information you provide while using the Service:**
- Your UPI ID — stored only as plain text, used solely to generate a payment QR code on your behalf. **We do not access, process, or have any visibility into your actual bank account, balance, or transaction history.**
- Project and business information: client brief details (brand, product, audience, campaign goals), pricing you set per stage, and your income/payment history.
- Files you upload: reference images, video clips, and other project deliverables.
- UPI transaction reference numbers (UTRs) you submit when requesting to purchase income credits.

**Automatically collected:** we use a session cookie to keep you signed in. We do not use any advertising or analytics tracking cookies or scripts — StagePay currently has no third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking integrated into the Service.

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information above to:

- operate your account and the Service, including generating your payment QR codes and public Showcase/pay pages;
- review and decide on your waitlist application;
- manually verify your UPI transaction ID when you request to purchase income credits;
- respond to support requests you send us;
- comply with applicable law, and protect the security and integrity of the Service.

We do not sell your personal information, and we do not use it for third-party advertising.

## 3. What Becomes Public

Some information you provide is **intentionally made public**, with no login required to view it:

- **Your Showcase page**: your name, profile photo, any work you choose to add, and your optional public contact link (Instagram, LinkedIn, or WhatsApp), are all visible to anyone with your Showcase link.
- **Your project pay/delivery links**: each shows your name, the relevant project/product details, and **your UPI ID rendered as a payment QR code**, visible to anyone with that specific link — this is necessary so your customer can pay you.

You are responsible for only sharing these links, and only adding Showcase content, that you're comfortable making public.

## 4. Third Parties We Work With

- **Cloudflare** — hosts our application, database, and file storage.
- **Google** — provides sign-in (OAuth) only; we do not use any other Google services to collect data about you.

We do not share your personal information with advertisers, data brokers, or any other third party for marketing purposes.

## 5. Data Retention & Deletion

- We retain your data for as long as your account remains active.
- **Deleting a project is immediate and permanent** — it removes that project's files, its content on your Showcase (if any), and its income records. This cannot be reversed (see our Terms, Section 8).
- **Account deletion**: there is currently no self-service option to delete your entire account. To request deletion of your account and associated data, email us at stagepay.support@gmail.com, and we will process your request except where we're required to retain certain records by law.

## 6. Security

- We never collect or store a password for your StagePay account — sign-in is entirely through Google.
- We take reasonable technical measures to protect your data, but **no method of storage or transmission is 100% secure**, and we cannot guarantee absolute security, particularly during the beta period referenced in our Terms.

## 7. Children's Privacy

The Service is not intended for use by anyone under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from anyone under 18. If you believe a minor has provided us with personal information, please contact us at stagepay.support@gmail.com and we will take steps to remove it.

## 8. Your Rights

Subject to applicable Indian law (including the Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023), you may request to:

- access the personal information we hold about you;
- correct inaccurate information;
- request deletion of your account and associated data (see Section 5).

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at stagepay.support@gmail.com.
