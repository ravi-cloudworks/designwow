## 7. Income Credits, Pricing & Refunds

- New approved accounts receive **10 free income credits**. Existing accounts at the time this pricing model was introduced may have been granted a different starting balance at our discretion.
- One income credit is consumed each time you price a new, previously-unpriced stage of work (opening a "receivable") — regardless of the amount you charge your customer for that stage. Renegotiating the price of a stage that is already open and unpaid does not consume an additional credit.
- Once your free credits are used, additional credits may be purchased in packs (currently 5 credits for ₹245, or 10 credits for ₹490 — subject to change per Section 3).
- **Credit purchases are made by paying StagePay's own UPI ID directly** — the same no-gateway model described in Section 5 applies to your payments to us. You must supply the correct **UPI transaction ID (UTR)** for your payment when submitting a credit purchase request.
- Credits are added to your account **only after we manually verify your UTR against the payment received.** An incorrect, invalid, already-used, or unverifiable UTR will delay processing, and **we may reject the request outright** at our discretion if it cannot be verified.
